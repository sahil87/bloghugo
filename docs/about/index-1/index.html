About

I am a freak, an atheist, a thinker, a problem sniffer, a workaholic, a music nerd, a free software advocate, a virus, a bathroom singer, a woman lover, a photographer, a chess player, an idiot, a clown, and alive.

I was born in 1987 at Ahmedabad, Gujrat (India). The part after that gets very confusing. So, I have organized the names of the places I have lived in (for myself):

Place Period School
Ahmedabad 7 yrs Thumblina (Nursery, KG1 KG2), St. Xavier Mirzapur(1st)
Assam 3 yrs Don Bosco, Holy Name School (2.5 to 5.5)
Rajahmundry 6 yrs St. Annes(5th to 6th), Kendriya Vidyalaya(7th to 10th)
Faridabad 2 yrs Delhi Public School, Faridabad
I completed my Bachelors of Technology (4 yrs) in Instrumentation and Control from National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli in 2008.

Currently I am an analyst developer at Goldman Sachs.
