About

The programmer, like the poet, works only slightly removed from pure thought-stuff. He builds his castles in the air, from air, creating by exertion of the imagination.
― Frederick P. Brooks Jr., Mythical Man-Month

Hi! I am Sahil Ahuja, a coder turned banker turned coder trying to climb up the ropes almost there! Known to be a freak, atheist, thinker, problem sniffer, workaholic, music nerd, free software advocate, bathroom singer, photographer, chess player, an idiot and alive.

My tech interests include Dev Ops, Web Technologies and VR. Current toolkit is primarily Emacs, Atom, WebStorm, zsh, nodejs, ES6/JSX, Ansible and Jenkins. Want to add Android to the toolkit next.

Learning how to play Violin and Piano with Sheet Music, trying to improve my Relative Pitch and going through online courses.

I graduated from NIT Trichy, did MBA from IIM-C with a knack for building systems from ground up. Co-founder/CTO of SportsCafe since mid-2015.
